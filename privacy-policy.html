ExtroPOS Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 7, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how ExtroPOS ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our point-of-sale application.

Information We Collect

Device Information

For license management purposes, we collect:

  • Unique device identifier (Android ID or Windows device ID)
  • Google account email (optional, for license binding)

Business Data

The app stores business-related data locally on your device:

  • Product catalogs
  • Transaction records
  • Customer information
  • Business settings

Cloud Synchronization (Optional)

If you choose to use cloud features:

  • Data is synchronized with Appwrite servers
  • Server location: Remote hosting (appwrite.extropos.org)

How We Use Your Information

License Management

  • Verify license validity
  • Prevent unauthorized use across multiple devices
  • Process in-app purchases

App Functionality

  • Store business data locally
  • Generate reports and analytics
  • Print receipts and reports

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

  • All business data is stored locally on your device
  • Uses SQLite database with optional encryption
  • PIN codes are encrypted using Flutter Secure Storage

Cloud Storage (Optional)

  • License binding data stored on Appwrite servers
  • Data encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Access controlled by API keys

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties, except as described below:

  • Google Play Services: For in-app purchases and license verification
  • Appwrite: For cloud synchronization (if enabled)
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights

Your Rights

Device Binding

Lifetime licenses are bound to a single device. You can unbind a license from within the app to transfer it to another device.

Data Deletion

  • You can delete all local data through the app's maintenance tools
  • Cloud data can be deleted by contacting support

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: support@extropos.org

Website: https://extropos.org

This privacy policy is compliant with Google Play Store requirements and applicable data protection laws.
