## What's New

### YouTube Integration for Customer Display

Display promotional videos, advertisements, or business content on the customer-facing screen when the POS is idle. The system intelligently switches between video playback and cart display based on activity.

**Key Behaviors:**

- ‚úÖ Video plays automatically when cart is empty

- ‚úÖ Video pauses when items are added to cart

- ‚úÖ Cart display takes priority during transactions

- ‚úÖ Video resumes when cart is cleared

- ‚úÖ Loops continuously until cart becomes active

---

## How to Use

### Quick Setup (3 Steps)

1. **Open Settings** ‚Üí **Dual Display Settings**

2. **Enable** "YouTube Video Display"

3. **Enter** YouTube URL ‚Üí **Save**

That's it! The video will start playing on the customer display when idle.

### Example URLs

```
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dQw4w9WgXcQ
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VIDEO_ID_HERE

```

## Known Limitations

1. **Requires Internet**: Videos stream from YouTube
2. **Single Video**: No playlist support (yet)
3. **Restart Required**: URL changes need vice display restart
4. **YouTube Restrictions**: Some videos may be geo-blocked

## Troubleshooting

### Video Not Playing?

- ‚úÖ Check YouTube enabled in settings

- ‚úÖ Ensure internet connection active
